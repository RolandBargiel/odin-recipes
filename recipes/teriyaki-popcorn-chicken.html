Teriyaki Popcorn Chicken

Go back to homepage.

Description

A family favorite from Hawai'i!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 cup of teriyaki sauce
  • 1 cup of breading
  • 1 egg

Cooking Instructions

  1. Start your rice in your rice cooker.
  2. Dice your chicken into 1/2 inch to 1 inch cubes.
  3. Break egg (with yolk) into mixing bowl
  4. Prepare another mixing bowl with breading in it.
  5. Dip diced chicken into egg, and then coat in breading.
  6. Heat a pot of oil on medium/medium-high.
  7. Carefully place breaded chicken into hot oil, cook until golden brown.
  8. Once golden brown, remove chicken and place on a paper towel to soak up excess oil.
  9. Toss finished chicken with teriyaki sauce.
  10. Serve over a bed of rice.
